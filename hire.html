Digital Project Specialist
(Research Assistant 1)

Search #: 495187
Work type: Full-time
Location: Storrs Campus
Categories: Research

Note

At the UConn DX Group, we're all about designing products that work for a wide variety of users. Topics of accessibility, universal design, and inclusion are critical to the creation of effective digital experiences. This means that our success depends on the combined perspective of team members from all backgrounds, worldviews and identities; it makes our work stronger. We intend to keep this core value at the forefront of our recruiting process.

Job Description

The UConn DX Group seeks to hire a Digital Project Specialist to join our team at the rank of Research Assistant 1. This position will make sure we’re helping our partners solve problems to the best of our ability.

This position is for someone with a wide range of skills and a strong desire to learn. You will work closely with our faculty directors, designers, web developers, and student staff, as well as with our clients and partners at the University of Connecticut, to ensure that our mission of “supporting research at UConn through digital innovation” is fulfilled. From day to day, your tasks will include facilitating stand-up meetings with student staff and directors, interfacing with clients and partners to translate feedback into actionable items for our production teams, and performing content reviews, audits, and updates to our digital products.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Work closely with our directors, other staff, and clients/partners to capture requirements for our various digital products;
  • Monitor workflow and status of project tasks and client requests and provide regular updates to managers and team via stand-up meetings and other frequent communications;
  • Assign tasks to student staff as appropriate;
  • Administer web pages and help implement new functionality for products hosted in Wordpress or other content management systems;
  • Provide Section 508 and other accessibility-related review on web pages and other digital content;
  • Provide training to non-technical staff and clients;
  • Help with strategic planning around ways to better support our clients;

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in design, liberal arts, business, or related field;
  • Prior coursework or professional experience in web design, digital marketing, or related field;
  • Prior coursework or professional experience in managing design and development projects;
  • Experience with WordPress or similar content management systems.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with HTML and CSS;
  • Experience with Agile software development and related processes.

Appointment Terms

This is a full-time, 12-month, annually renewable position. Telecommuting arrangements are possible. The typical workday will be normal EST business hours. We offer an industry-competitive salary including a robust benefits package.
