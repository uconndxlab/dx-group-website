Digital Experience Group

Supporting Research at UConn Through Digital Innovation.

With its multidisciplinary team composed of Digital Media & Design faculty members Brian Daley, Joel Salisbury, and Michael Vertefeuille, and assistance from staff, graduate students, and paid interns, the DX Group offers collective decades of experience in web design, UI / UX design, application development, and technological innovation.

The DX Group uses an Agile approach during the concept, prototype and full-scale deployment phases of app development. Since this methodology mirrors that of industry leaders, the DX Group can adapt to new challenges. All of the DX Group’s services emphasize the most basic principle they teach their students: to utilize the newest and most cutting-edge approaches to digital design.

Capabilities

App Development

The DX Group has worked with researchers across from UConn campuses to build mobile apps, web apps, and other digital products in support of numerous varied research activities.

UX/UI Design

Our team can collaborate on rapid UI/UX prototyping of digital products and redesigns of existing web and mobile apps.

User Research

The DX Group team can conduct needfinding, user surveys, focus groups, and UX testing for digital products of all types.

Other Technology Innovation

Have an idea for a digital product that doesn’t quite fit the descriptions you’ve seen so far? Our team can provide technical consultation on installations, large-format digital experiences, non-touch interactive projects, and more.

Fees

The Digital Experience Group is a University service center with the ability to conduct internal billing as well as participate in grant proposal submissions. Rates are as follows:

Faculty Oversight/Development $120 per hour
Application development (professional) $100 per hour
Graduate Assistant $50 per hour
Student Design $20 per hour
Student Development $20 per hour
Hosting Services $ varies
Equipment Fees (for research relating to custom development) $ varies
