Digital Experience Group

Supporting Research at UConn Through Digital Innovation.

With its multidisciplinary team composed of Digital Media & Design faculty members Brian Daley, Joel Salisbury, and Michael Vertefeuille, and assistance from staff, graduate students, and paid interns, the DX Group offers collective decades of experience in web design, UI / UX design, application development, and technological innovation.

As a sibling of the UConn DMD Digital Experience Lab, a research lab homed by the Digital Media & Design Department, we utilize the latest research, and emerging thought leadership in UX Design, App Development, and Digital Product Design.

The DX Group uses an Agile approach during the concept, prototype and full-scale deployment phases of app development. Projects

WellScan

What if food banks could better know the quality of their inventory? In collaboration with UConn's Rudd Center on Food Policy and Obesity, and with support from Partnership For Healthier America, Feeding America, and others, the DX Lab has developed a suite of web/mobile applications product for use by food banks, food pantries, and other organizations for ranking the nutritional quality of their inventory.

This application is housed, licensed and serviced at the University of Connecticut, and includes the WellScan Global nutrition database, WellScan Connect API, WelScan Mini scanning app.
