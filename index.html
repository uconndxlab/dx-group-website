Supporting Research at UConn Through Digital Innovation.

The Digital Experience Group provides the UConn research community with access to high quality app development, web design, UI/UX design, and other technology innovation services.

Latest News

  • 20 September, 2022 | The DX Group has now joined forces with Squared Labs. More info to come!

Capabilities

App Development

The DX Group has worked with researchers across from UConn campuses to build mobile apps, web apps, and other digital products in support of numerous varied research activities.

Web/UX Design

Our team can work with yours to rapidly prototype and deploy beautiful and usable websites and apps.

User Research

The DX Group team can conduct needfinding, user surveys, focus groups, and UX testing for digital products of all types.

Other Technology Innovation

Have an idea for a digital product that doesn’t quite fit the descriptions you’ve seen so far? Our team can provide technical consultation on installations, large-format digital experiences, non-touch interactive projects, and more.

Fees

The Digital Experience Group is a University service center with the ability to conduct internal billing as well as participate in grant proposal submissions. Rates are as follows:

Faculty Oversight/Development $150 per hour
Application development (professional) $105 per hour
Graduate Assistant $51 per hour
Student Design $25 per hour
Student Development $25 per hour
Hosting Services $ varies
Equipment Fees (for research relating to custom development) $ varies
